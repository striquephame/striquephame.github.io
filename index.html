WHO IS STRIQUE PHAME

STRIQUE PHAME aka DAT PEKI BOI is a Ghanaian Music Artiste, (Afrobeats & Highlife) an energetic performer and a truly versatile Musician. He was Born in Tema but Raised in Peki-Avetile in the Volta part of Ghana.

Born Adzamli Selassi Frank, Strique Phame loved music as a kid, but truly discovered his music potential at age fifteen during his Junior High School (JHS) days. He was widely influenced by songs from his parent’s collection - Lucky Dube, Kojo Antwi, Paa Bobo, and Amakye Dede. At age 15 he started listening to Lil Wayne, V.I.P, Buk Bak, 4x4, Awilo Longomba and other hip-hop, highlife and francophone musicians.

From Peki to the world, carrying Ewe soul with every verse. This is music for the people who never quit. Welcome to the PHAMEily.
