WHO IS STRIQUE PHAME

STRIQUE PHAME aka DAT PEKI BOI is a Ghanaian Music Artiste, (Afrobeats & Highlife) an energetic performer and a truly versatile Musician. He was Born in Tema but Raised in Peki-Avetile in the volta part of Ghana to be precise. Born Adzamli Selassi Frank, Strique Phame loved music as a kid, but truly discovered his music potential at age fifteen during his Junior High School (JHS) days. He was widely influenced by songs from his parent’s collection of music from the likes of Lucky Dube, Kojo Antwi,Paa Bobo, and Amakye Dede. At age '15' he started listening to the likes of Lilwayne,Kojo Antwi,Vip,Buk Bak,4x4,Awilo Longomba and a host of other hip-hop,highlife and francophone musicians as a result of the influences of his community . over the years, has released some quality videos to most of his songs you might want to subscribe to his Youtube Channel "Strique PhamePK" to check them out. Please do well to watch this videos as there is more to come. Thank You. From Peki to the world, carrying Ewe soul with every verse. This is music for the people who never quit.
